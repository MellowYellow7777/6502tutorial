On Data Representation

8-bit values:
Binary: 	00000000 - 11111111
Hexadecimal:	00 - ff
Decimal: 	0 - 255

16-bit values:
Binary: 	00000000 00000000 - 11111111 11111111
Hexadecimal:	0000 - ffff
Decimal:	0 - 65535



Assembly Notation:
Binary:		%01111111
Hexadecimal:	$7f
Decimal: 	127

Javascript Syntax:
Binary:		0b01111111
Hexadecimal:	0x7f
Decimal: 	127
